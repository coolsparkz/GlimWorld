# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

GlimWorld is a community built on respect, collaboration, and creativity. We're committed to providing a welcoming and supportive environment for all Gimkit Creative builders, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

### ✅ Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful and supportive** — treat everyone with kindness
- **Share knowledge freely** — help others learn and grow
- **Give constructive feedback** — focus on being helpful, not critical
- **Celebrate others' work** — recognize and appreciate community contributions
- **Stay on topic** — keep discussions relevant to Gimkit Creative and building

### 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment or bullying** — including offensive comments, personal attacks, or intimidation
- **Spam or excessive self-promotion** — outside of sharing your own creations
- **Plagiarism** — claiming others' work as your own
- **Sharing inappropriate content** — keep it safe for all ages
- **Disruptive behavior** — trolling, derailing conversations, or intentionally causing conflict

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all GlimWorld spaces, including:
- The GlimWorld Padlet
- GitHub repository and discussions
- Any official GlimWorld communication channels

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:
- Use our [contact form](https://forms.office.com/r/kFyH7TXtQJ)
- Provide details about the incident
- Reports will be handled confidentially
