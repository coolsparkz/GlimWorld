What is GlimWorld?

GlimWorld is a builder-focused community for Gimkit Creative creators.

It's where people come together to:

  • Share their creations — maps, games, builds
  • Learn techniques — tips, tricks, how-tos
  • Troubleshoot — get help when stuck
  • Collaborate — work together on projects

Our mission: "Build, share, collaborate — and keep it real."

How to Join

Getting started with GlimWorld is easy! Follow these simple steps to join our active builder community:

  1. Visit the GlimWorld Padlet
  2. Review the Community Rules & FAQ to understand our guidelines
  3. Start sharing your creations and connecting with other builders

Community Guidelines

We maintain a positive, inclusive environment for all members. Here's what we stand for:

Core Values:

  • ✅Be respectful and supportive of all community members
  • 🎨Share your builds, tutorials, and help others learn
  • 💡Encourage collaboration and creative problem-solving
  • 🚫No spam or self-promotion outside of your creations
  • 📚Keep discussions constructive and on-topic

Read the full Community Rules & FAQ on the GlimWorld Padlet for detailed guidelines.
